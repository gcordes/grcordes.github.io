If Art Could Speak

Base Conversation

Interviews with Art

Art does not speak verbal language, therefore the language used may come as music or sound. They are interpretations of what it could be like to talk to a viewer like you.

Every piece has a different sound or reverberation, which eludes to a different subject. Some are hyper and some are peaceful. There is no saying what art will say to each individual.

Sometimes art can sound like a lot of chitter chatter and it does not make any sense. We are fighting to get the spotlight, and it almost seems repetitive.

Sometimes music is an artistic language in itself. Listening to it takes you to a whole different place emotionally. Try to imagine what sort of art piece would best go with the two following selections.

Music varies, which leads to different thoughts and emotions.
